About Us

Greenwave is a team from Minerva University focused on combating Climate Change by focusing on Solar array adoption in developing countries like Brazil. ​​ Our platform is an online map platform that connects Nicaragua residential installers with Bay Area solar developers. This solution utilizes the position of the residential installers within their country. They can spread accurate information about solar tech, increasing reputation and leading to more adoption. Our solution is an online platform that provides the residential installer with the latest developments and allows the developer to spread their tech abroad through direct connections with local installers.
